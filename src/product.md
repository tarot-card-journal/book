# Product

Tarot Card Journal is the most user friendly personal tarot card reader.

By writing your thoughts and feelings in a journal daily the AI can use
tarot card readings to help you understand yourself and your life better.

## Features

- **Daily Journaling**: Write your thoughts and feelings every day.
- **AI Tarot Card Readings**: Get personalized tarot card readings informed by
  your journal entries.
- **Amazing Tarot Card Art**: Beautiful illustrations of tarot cards created by
  professional artists.
- **Learn Tarot**: Learn to be a tarot card reader with our interactive lessons.
